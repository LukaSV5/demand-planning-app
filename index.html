Behind every accurate forecast is the right model for the right product

Different products behave differently — some follow strong seasonal cycles, others respond to external market signals, and many are too complex for a single algorithm to capture. The workspace runs a seven-model lineup — from a Seasonal Naive benchmark through SES, Theta, and Auto-ETS to a Top-3 Ensemble — and the three spotlighted below deliver the best results across the widest range of demand scenarios. Best Fit evaluates every model against each SKU's historical pattern with walk-forward validation and selects the one with the lowest error automatically.

Harmonic Regression

Prophet-Style

Built for seasonality & real-world messiness

A Prophet-style decomposition — trend plus Fourier seasonal harmonics fitted by regression. Captures recurring seasonal shape with minimal tuning, even on noisy histories.

Strengths
Flexible multi-wave seasonality
Stable on short or noisy histories
80% prediction intervals included
Classical Statistical

Auto-ETS

The flagship: damped-trend Holt-Winters

Damped-trend Holt-Winters with smoothing parameters auto-tuned per SKU. Level, trend, and seasonality adapt to each product, and every state is mathematically interpretable.

Strengths
Auto-tuned per SKU, zero manual config
Damped trend avoids runaway forecasts
Top walk-forward accuracy overall
Machine Learning

Global XGBoost

One model trained across every SKU

A single gradient-boosting model trained across all SKUs at once. Lag, calendar, and SKU-level features let sparse products borrow strength from patterns learned portfolio-wide.

Strengths
Learns across the entire portfolio
Captures complex variable interactions
Strong on sparse, irregular SKUs

All 7 models are evaluated on MAE, RMSE, and MAPE under walk-forward (rolling-origin) validation — Best Fit selects the top performer per SKU, with 80% prediction intervals on every forecast.
