Honest accuracy

Re-fit from scratch. Score blind. Repeat.

Every model is re-trained — grid searches, auto-tuning, everything — on expanding windows of your history, then graded only on months it never saw. Errors are pooled across origins and horizons into one WAPE and MASE you can actually plan against.

In-sample fit is shown too, but labeled what it is: optimistic by construction.

From CSV to defensible order quantity
in four steps.

No onboarding call. No implementation project. The demo dataset loads in one click if you'd rather not bring your own.

01 · CONNECT

Drop in your CSVs.

Sales history, stock levels, suppliers, purchase orders — columns are auto-mapped, every assignment re-mappable, multi-file add supported. Or press Load demo dataset and skip straight to step 2.

02 · AUDIT

Watch every SKU get backtested.

A Web Worker walk-forward-validates all seven models on every SKU in the background while you browse. A progress chip streams results, and each winner is cached so its drawer opens instantly.

03 · DECIDE

The routed model is pre-selected: switch between all 7, click any leaderboard row to override, read the 80% band, stretch the history horizon, export the PNG for your S&OP deck.

The replenishment workspace prices every row off the winning forecast: To-Order at MOQ, reorder points, cover days, health status, projected replenish dates — Create PO and Export a click away.

Headline metrics throughout: WAPE and MASE under walk-forward (rolling-origin) validation — re-fit per origin, errors pooled across origins × horizon, MASE benchmarked against seasonal naive. MAE and RMSE reported alongside. In-sample fit is displayed but explicitly demoted.

Behind every accurate forecast is the right model for the right product

Different products behave differently — some follow strong seasonal cycles, others respond to external market signals, and many are too complex for a single algorithm to capture. The workspace runs a seven-model lineup — from a Seasonal Naive benchmark through SES, Theta, and Auto-ETS to a Top-3 Ensemble — and the three spotlighted below deliver the best results across the widest range of demand scenarios. Best Fit evaluates every model against each SKU's historical pattern with walk-forward validation and selects the one with the lowest error automatically.

Harmonic Regression

Prophet-Style

Built for seasonality & real-world messiness

A Prophet-style decomposition — trend plus Fourier seasonal harmonics fitted by regression. Captures recurring seasonal shape with minimal tuning, even on noisy histories.

Strengths
Flexible multi-wave seasonality
Stable on short or noisy histories
80% prediction intervals included
Classical Statistical

Auto-ETS

The flagship: damped-trend Holt-Winters

Damped-trend Holt-Winters with smoothing parameters auto-tuned per SKU. Level, trend, and seasonality adapt to each product, and every state is mathematically interpretable.

Strengths
Auto-tuned per SKU, zero manual config
Damped trend avoids runaway forecasts
Top walk-forward accuracy overall
Machine Learning

Global XGBoost

One model trained across every SKU

A single gradient-boosting model trained across all SKUs at once. Lag, calendar, and SKU-level features let sparse products borrow strength from patterns learned portfolio-wide.

Strengths
Learns across the entire portfolio
Captures complex variable interactions
Strong on sparse, irregular SKUs
